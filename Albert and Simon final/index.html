Objective

The goal of this game is to bypass the dungeons and collect all the treasure as Jeremy the Miner without losing all of your lives.
In the game, you are stuck in a dungeon without your pickaxe.
To beat each level, you must find your pickaxe and then mine your way through the rocks to grab the treasure.
At the start of the game, you have three lives, but if you run into a zombie, one of your lives will be lost.
If all three of your are lost or if you run out of time, you lose!

Controls

Use the arrow keys to move.
To pause the game, press the escape key or the pause button at the top of the game.
There are four difficulties which determine how much time you have:
Easy (120s), Medium (75s), Hard (40s) and Insane (20s).
The default is Easy.
